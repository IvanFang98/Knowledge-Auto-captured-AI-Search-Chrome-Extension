How to Use:

  • Navigate to any webpage

  • Text will be extracted and saved automatically
  • Use Similarity search to find related content
  • Use Keywords search for exact word matches

Similarity Search:

Uses TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency) to find semantically similar content. Perfect for discovering related articles and preparing content for NotebookLM integration.
